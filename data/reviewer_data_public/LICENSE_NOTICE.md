# Data Release Notice

This package is a public academic-review release. E1 numeric rows are de-identified experiment-derived data, E2/E3 records are sanitized experiment evidence, and the system fixture is fully synthetic.

The package may be used for peer review, verification of the associated paper, teaching, and non-commercial academic research with attribution. Re-identification, linkage to real entities, commercial profiling, and reconstruction or redistribution of the confidential source database are prohibited. No rights to the source database, original identifiers, or unpublished mappings are granted.

The data are provided without warranty. Normalized feature combinations may remain structurally distinctive; users must preserve the included privacy, provenance, limitation, and checksum documentation.
