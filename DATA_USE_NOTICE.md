# Data Use Notice

## Scope

This notice applies to `data/reviewer_data_public/` and its ZIP archive. It does not apply to the reconstructed source code, which is covered by the MIT License.

The package contains de-identified, normalized values derived from historical experiments; sanitized aggregate or repeated experiment outcomes; and clearly marked synthetic fixtures. It does not contain the confidential source database, raw bibliographic records, real display names, credentials, or an identity crosswalk.

## Permitted use

The data may be used for peer review, verification of the associated paper, teaching, and non-commercial academic research, provided that the repository and associated paper are cited and the privacy boundary is preserved.

## Restrictions

Users must not:

1. attempt to re-identify entities or link public identifiers to real people, institutions, publications, or source-database identifiers;
2. represent synthetic fixtures as historical measurements or real entities;
3. use this package to reconstruct, redistribute, or claim rights over the confidential source database;
4. remove provenance, privacy, limitation, or checksum documentation when redistributing the package for an authorized academic purpose;
5. use the data for commercial profiling, surveillance, or decisions about identifiable individuals or institutions.

## Warranty and residual risk

The data are provided for academic verification without warranty. Normalized feature combinations may remain structurally distinctive even after direct identifiers are removed. Users are responsible for complying with applicable law, research ethics, and institutional requirements.
